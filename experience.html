My Working Experience

My experience of working really taught me to be tough and mature in life. There were so many things hapenned for a month I working there. How people actually can treat you as bad as they can, we could not assume people will always do kind with us no matter how kind us to them. That time I was 17 with ZERO experience of working anywhere and I was hoping my first experience will be the best things I ever felt but I realized that I was wrong. Fortunately, there are few things I think it really helps me today by working there which are it improves my communication skill and how in handling my anger as well as be kind with people who deserve it.
